Celebrating Kenyan Craftsmanship

At Rosebellah Fashion Studio, we tell stories through fabric — stories of heritage, elegance, and modern Kenyan style. Based in Nakuru, our atelier merges timeless tailoring with innovative design to create garments that empower confidence and self-expression.

From intricately hand-sewn bridal gowns to made-to-measure suits and ready-to-wear collections, each creation is crafted with precision, sustainability, and a deep respect for African artistry. We source fabrics locally and collaborate with skilled artisans, ensuring every stitch reflects quality and pride in Kenyan design.

Explore Our Craft

Our Story & Mission

Founded by Rosebellah Otieno, a visionary Kenyan designer, the studio stands for creativity, inclusivity, and the empowerment of local artisans. Our mission is to redefine African fashion — blending elegance with authenticity and elevating tailoring into an art form.

Our Commitment

Every garment that leaves our studio reflects care, precision, and the passion of Kenyan craftsmanship. We believe in creating fashion that fits every story — from corporate professionals to brides, grooms, and anyone who values originality and elegance.

  • 100% Custom-Fitted Designs
  • Locally Sourced & Sustainable Materials
  • Collaboration with Kenyan Artisans

Our Vision

To become Kenya’s leading bespoke fashion house — where innovation meets tradition. A space where modern design and cultural roots coexist beautifully, empowering every client to look and feel their best.

Whether for a runway, boardroom, or wedding aisle, we aim to create pieces that inspire confidence and celebrate Africa’s creative heartbeat.
